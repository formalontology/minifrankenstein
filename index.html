Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to my digital edition of Chapter 7A from Mary Shelley's Frankenstein manuscript (Bodleian MS. Abinger c.56). This edition provides a glimpse into the creative process behind one of literature's most enduring works, showcasing both Mary Shelley's original text and Percy Shelley's editorial contributions. Created as part of the Text as Data II course at the University of Antwerp, this digital edition features detailed TEI-XML encoded transcriptions of ten manuscript pages, complete with annotations of additions, deletions, and editorial markings. Readers can explore the manuscript pages through high-quality IIIF images while examining the transcriptions that highlight the collaborative writing process between Mary and Percy Shelley.
